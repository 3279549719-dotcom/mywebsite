# Personal Site — Research

## Reference

**Primary**: [Colin Moy personal site](https://colin-moy.webflow.io/)

- Like: minimal three-section layout, clear hierarchy, comfortable whitespace, good mobile layout
- Borrow: hero states who I am; content split into clear blocks

## Positioning and style

- Style: clean business-card look; text and blocks first, images support the story
- Core experience: visitor understands who I am, what I enjoy, and how to contact me within ~3 minutes of scrolling

## Content structure

1. **Hero**: avatar + name + one-line intro; button or scroll to About
2. **Interests**: museum visits, hiking, manga localization; card click expands or shows detail
3. **Contact**: Instagram and email only

## Target users

- New friends and potential collaborators
- Scenario: share link after connecting on WeChat or social apps

## Conclusions

- Full-page scroll; all assets local under the project
- No blog, comments, or external APIs in v1

## Out of scope

- Login / registration
- CMS
- Comments or likes
- External database
- Social links other than Instagram and email
